# Highlight options

---

## Simple format
> **Note**
> This is a note

> **Warning**
> This is a warning

---

## Extended types
> [!NOTE]
> NOTE

> [!TIP]
> TIP

> [!IMPORTANT]
> IMPORTANT

> [!WARNING]
> WARNING

> [!CAUTION]
> CAUTION

---

## Nested
> [!Note] This also includes a title
> Note body
> > [!Hint]- Hint title
> > This is hint body
