# Highlight options

---

## Simple format
> **Note**
> This is a note

> **Warning**
> This is a warning

---

## Extended types
> [!NOTE] Note title
> NOTE

> [!TIP] Tip title
> TIP

> [!IMPORTANT] Important title
> IMPORTANT

> [!WARNING] Warning title
> WARNING

> [!CAUTION] Caution title
> CAUTION

---

## Nested
> [!Note] This also includes a title
> Note body
> > [!Hint]- Hint title
> > This is hint body
